About Us

Our goal is to help you have fun with your friends.

In our website you can create or join a room.
While in a room, you can share videos and watch them together with your friends.


During 2020, the Covid-19 Virus took over our lives and made us stay at home.
All the lockdowns and quarantines left us stranded without the ability
to meet up with all of our friends.

Our goal is to allow you and the people you care about to spend time together
and have fun together watching videos to pass time during this crazy time.
